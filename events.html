Events & Activities

School & Classroom Ideas

  • Design challenge: teams design a habitat and present architecture, mass budgets, and resource flows.
  • Rocket-building: teach basics of propulsion and aerodynamics using safe model rockets or water rockets.
  • Satellite tracker workshop: introduce how satellites orbit and how to track passes using free tools.

Public events & exhibits

  • Stargazing nights in parks or at observatories with guided tours of the sky.
  • Public talks: host researchers, engineers, or a remote astronaut Q&A.
  • Interactive exhibits: life-support demos, VR experiences of space habitats, and model displays.

Online & community events

  • Virtual hackathons focusing on climate monitoring, satellite-data analysis, or resource-optimization.
  • Photo and art contests with space-themed categories.
  • Live-streamed panel discussions between international participants.

How to organize & register

Register your event with the World Space Week Association (link on the Resources page). Prepare an event brief (audience, goals, materials, safety notes), promote via social media, and partner with local schools, museums or clubs.

Tip: keep events accessible and inclusive — provide captions for virtual talks and ADA-friendly venues for in-person gatherings.
