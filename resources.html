Resources & Further Reading

Official & Organizational

  • World Space Week Association — event registration, toolkits, promotional assets.
  • United Nations Office for Outer Space Affairs (UNOOSA) — global policy, UN events.

Space Agencies & Educational

  • NASA — educational resources, lesson plans, media.
  • European Space Agency (ESA) — missions, educational activities.
  • Space foundations, planetariums, and local observatories also provide region-specific material.

Tools & Data

  • Satellite data portals and open datasets for Earth observation (check national space agencies and Copernicus).
  • Open-source satellite tracking apps (for demonstrations and stargazing planning).
  • STEM education repositories for lesson plans and printable materials.

Credits & Licensing

When using images, videos, or third-party content, check licensing and attribution requirements. Use openly-licensed assets (Creative Commons) for classroom and public displays where possible.
