World Space Week 2025

October 4–10 — Theme: Living in Space

Explore the Cosmos — World Space Week 2025

World Space Week is the largest annual celebration of space, highlighting how space science and technology benefit life on Earth and enable our future beyond it.

Discover this year’s theme: Living in Space

What is World Space Week?

Declared by the United Nations in 1999, World Space Week is observed every year from October 4 to October 10. The dates commemorate the launch of Sputnik 1 (4 Oct 1957) and the entry into force of the Outer Space Treaty (10 Oct 1967).

Highlights

  • Thousands of events in 90+ countries.
  • Classroom programs, public exhibits, virtual talks, and stargazing nights.
  • Focus on STEM education and international cooperation.
