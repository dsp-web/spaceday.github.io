2025 Theme — Living in Space

Summary

The 2025 theme examines what it takes for humans to live and thrive beyond Earth. It emphasizes habitat engineering, life support systems, human health and psychology, resource recycling, and sustainable mission design.

Core topics

  • Habitat Design — inflatable modules, 3D-printed structures, underground/lava-tube concepts.
  • Life Support — air/water recycling, closed-loop life support systems, food production (hydroponics/vertical farming).
  • Human Factors — countermeasures for microgravity, radiation shielding, psychological well-being, crew autonomy.
  • In-Situ Resource Utilization (ISRU) — using local materials (regolith, ice) to make propellant, water, and building material.
  • Sustainability — minimizing launch mass and waste, designing repairable systems, environmental stewardship.

Educational activities & ideas

  • Design a Mars habitat project: materials, layout, resource budgets.
  • Calculate a life-support mass budget (air, water, food) for a crew of 4 for 180 days.
  • Simulate radiation shielding using different materials and thicknesses.
  • Build a small hydroponic system and track plant growth under different light conditions.
